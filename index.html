ABOUT US

We are one of the earliest incubator start-up in Indonesia, found in 2017 by Vinsensius Boido Simarmata, our company help start-up company to live and grow bigger and can fly to their “MOON”. Kosmonaut now is the most famous Indonesian leading incubator start-up and the only one in Indonesia that's have a partnership with international organization like FOWS, OIS, and IOAS.

WHY US?

We are better than other competitors

We won the best startup incubator award for 5 consecutive years (2018-2021) and we have a unique program that no one else has that has been tested with successful results

Better programs and services

With our unique programs such as awakening and balance that have been researched for 5 years, we are sure and certain that we have a unique program and services combined in a very unique way.

Flexibility in our Incubator
